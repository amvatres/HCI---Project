Terms and Privacy
1. Why do I have problems while trying to buy ticket?

Problems with buying ticket may be a result of incorrect payment information. If this is the case, you will be provided with toastr message. Also, try checking if your credit card is valid.

2. Why can't I see movies in categories?

Movies are not displayed on your menu because they are not part of our system yet. We are in process of updating our platform. You may expect to see movies in the next version of Conference Organizer.

3. Why do I have a problem while signing up?

This problem may be caused by invalid user credentials. Please, check if information that you entered can be validated. For example, your password must contain one upper case letter, one special character and must be at least 6 characters long.
